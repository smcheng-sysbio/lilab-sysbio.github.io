Research Areas

The Li laboratory is focused on the development and application of robust and bioinformatics algorithms to elucidate gene regulatory mechanisms in complex traits and diseases such as cancer, which further expected to translate to public health practice.

Genetic Basis of human diseases


Genome-wide association studies have identified thousands of noncoding variants that are statistically associated with human traits and diseases. However, the functional interpretation of these variants remains a significant challenge.
