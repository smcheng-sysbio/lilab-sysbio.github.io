Assistant Researcher

Xudong Zou, PhD

Xudong Zou received his Ph.D in Bioinformatics from Peking University in 2018. His research interests currently focus on: 1) Functional interpretation of human non-coding variants especially the functional rare variants (MAF<1%) and identification of disease-associated rare variations; 2) Mechanisms and evolution of transcriptional and post-transcriptional (alternative splicing and alternative polyadenylation) regulation.
